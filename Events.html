Socially Distanced Spring Walk 22/09/2020

Written by NaN

Our first meetup after a long pandemic induced break. Welcoming back our dues paying members and guests. R100 for non-members.

This walk will take place at the Wild's and focus both on the local history of the area as well as using the view of the skyline to discuss the rapid growth of urban Johannesburg. It take about one and half hour and will be finished by open and distanced picnic lunch on the greens.

Meet at 10:30am in the parking lot.

Rhodes Park Walk & Meetup 18/04/2021

Written by Simon Wessels

Starting off the new season of walking tours of east Joburg, we will be meeting this Sunday just outside the bowls club at Rhodes Park. We will be looking at some old houses of historical value in the area, passing by our office in Bez Valley for short discussion. Hosted by our resident expert on historical archeitecture and board member Mila Botha Simon Wessels.

We will then end up back at the bowls club for drinks. Walk should last about three hours with plenty of breaks. Free to members and R100 for non-members.
